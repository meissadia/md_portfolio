Posifocus Web is Live!

Launch App Visit Site

Posifocus is a mentality, supported by an app, that helps you not only organize all of your obligations and responsibilities, but it helps you get in the right state of mind to stay focused on what is most important and be exceptionally productive with your time. So rather than just surviving the daily grind, you will be thriving in a life full of purpose and meaning!

NBA stats for your Ruby projects.

Download Visit Repo

HoopScrape is a Ruby gem that allows you to easily integrate logistic and statistical information about your favorite basketball teams in your own development projects. It's versatile too, providing data access via Structs for dot notation, Hashes for quick ActiveRecord integration, or String arrays.

Take the hassle out of managing your queues!

DraftKing for Tumblr

Download Visit Repo

DraftKing for Tumblr is a command line utility that takes the hassle out of managing your posts by allowing you to automate a number of tasks for your Drafts and Queue including: adding tags, stripping previous comments, and moving Drafts to your Queue. Visit the repo for information on the latest release or to file a bug report!
